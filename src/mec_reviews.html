Last minute purchase did the job
Date:April 6, 2014
I purchased this since I didn't have anything appropriate to wear for a run this weekend. I got the olive oil colour. It is one of those bright but not blinding colours that would be great for days with little sunlight. With a touque and gloves it was perfect for a -5C race day. Good for a light wind, but you may need a shell if the wind picks up.
The interior is soft. The sleeves are long and on the skinny side. My arms are on the long side and the fold over mitt thing doesn't quite work for me. I found the body length to be fine. I just wish it had a second pocket for us lefties.
The zipper is small and may require a bit more concentration to do up, but it seems sturdy. We'll see how this holds up to washing and use.
Pros: Comfortable, Breathable
